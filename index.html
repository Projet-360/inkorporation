Jupiter

This planet mean the exploration, a trip to the unknow, an expansion by the spiritual access. Jupiter gives the permission to go beyond the rules, discover other cultures and thinkings. His determination, optimism and spirituality lead him on long journeys.

MARS

This planet represents the power of action. Its representative is the god of war "Arès". Blood flows in abundance. In the body as well as on the field. Mars holds the hatred but also the primary side of our survival. If he masters and manages these dark sides, he becomes a source of productive energy. The chosen style is "hard electro music". It starts with violence but will end in the balance and control of it. The fighting spirit, the competition, the leader, the warrior.
